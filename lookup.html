Do you really know how much of your info is online?

The availability of public information on the internet has grown significantly with the rise of social media platforms, online directories, and data aggregation services. Personal details such as full names, email addresses, phone numbers, and even home addresses can often be found online, either through public records or information shared voluntarily. This data is frequently collected, stored, and sold by third-party companies, making it accessible to anyone with the right tools or resources. While this can be convenient for legitimate purposes like reconnecting with old friends or verifying identities, it also poses significant privacy risks.
